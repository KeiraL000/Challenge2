About me

Hello! My name is Keira Lobatos, I have worked at Target for about five years now and it's been great! I feel a sense of satisfaction going into work, and knowing what I do will make a difference in how the store runs and looks. I also have a bit of experience in the welding field. I have taken a class for welding and have my own welder to work on projects with. I'm very creative in many ways and one of my favorite things to do is draw. I also enjoy nature immensely. One of my favorite things to do with my friends and even alone is mushroom hunting. There's always a good feeling finding a special type of mushroom and being able to identify with your own knowledge what type it is. I feel that way about many things and I've realized I put some of those feelings into my work aswell.
